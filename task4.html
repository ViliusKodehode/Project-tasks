Most visited destinations by international tourist arrivals

France

France, a country renowned for its rich history and cultural heritage, is a top tourist destination worldwide. From the iconic Eiffel Tower in Paris to the charming French Riviera, there's something for everyone. Explore ancient castles, vineyards, and picturesque villages while indulging in world-class cuisine and fine wines. Whether you're seeking romance, adventure, or relaxation, France offers an unforgettable experience.

Spain

Spain, another European gem, boasts a diverse landscape and vibrant culture. From the bustling cities of Madrid and Barcelona to the stunning beaches of the Costa del Sol, there's a wide range of attractions to discover. Explore ancient Roman ruins, Gothic cathedrals, and Moorish architecture while immersing yourself in the lively Spanish way of life. Indulge in delicious tapas, flamenco dancing, and bullfighting, all of which are integral parts of Spain's rich heritage.
